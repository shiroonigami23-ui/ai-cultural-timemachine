# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of the AI Cultural Time Machine seriously. If you discover a vulnerability, please report it to us as soon as possible.

### How to Report

We encourage responsible disclosure. Please do **NOT** open a public GitHub issue.

Instead, send an email to [INSERT SECURITY CONTACT EMAIL HERE] with the following information:

*   **Description**: A clear and concise description of the vulnerability.
*   **Steps to Reproduce**: Detailed steps to reproduce the vulnerability.
*   **Impact**: Describe the potential impact of the vulnerability.
*   **Proof of Concept (Optional)**: Any relevant code, screenshots, or network logs that demonstrate the vulnerability.

### Our Response

*   We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 3 business days.
*   We will investigate the reported vulnerability promptly.
*   We will keep you informed of our progress and any remediation plans.
*   We will notify you once the vulnerability has been resolved.

### Guidelines for Responsible Disclosure

*   Do not disclose the vulnerability publicly until it has been resolved.
*   Do not access or modify data that does not belong to you.
*   Do not interrupt or degrade our services.
*   Do not attempt social engineering, phishing, or physical attacks.

We appreciate your help in keeping AI Cultural Time Machine secure!
